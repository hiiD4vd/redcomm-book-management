# AI Usage Statement

In the spirit of transparency and resourcefulness, AI assistance was utilized during the development of this project to accelerate workflows and ensure best practices.

## How AI Was Leveraged

1. **Scaffolding & Boilerplate:** 
   AI was used to quickly generate boilerplate code, such as standard CRUD controller methods, FormRequests, and database migrations. This allowed me to focus more on architectural decisions and UX rather than typing repetitive syntax.

2. **Tailwind Design Iteration:**
   AI helped translate abstract design ideas (specifically the "Neobrutalism" design language) into concrete Tailwind CSS utility classes. It assisted in calculating precise shadow offsets and hover micro-animations without needing to write custom CSS from scratch.

3. **Dummy Data Localization:**
   AI was utilized to quickly generate realistic, localized Indonesian bio text and book descriptions for the Database Seeders.

4. **Testing Setup:**
   AI assisted in scaffolding the initial structure for the PHPUnit Feature tests and Vitest component tests, ensuring that edge cases (like testing missing required fields for 422 errors) were covered efficiently.

*By treating AI as an advanced pair-programmer, I was able to deliver a robust, fully tested, and visually striking application well within the standard time constraints.*
